How does MinePass work ?

For Players

Players sign up and are issued a passbook and profile

Your passbook keeps track of the worlds you visit, and the type of pass you have.
Your profile stores frequently requested information to simplify world applications.

Players explore for new worlds and apply for passes

MinePass has a built in search and ranking system. To join a world visitors passes can be issued instantly (if enabled), and other pass applications are streamlined with custom fields.

Once an application is approved the player is synced to server whitelists automatically.
Server plugins also catalog mods for easy filtering and searching.

Each world's portal is available to valid players

The portal lists servers and provides collaborative and administration functions depending on the type of player.

For World Owners

1. World Operators install the MinePass plugin and input their world details

Plugins (or wrappers) actively enforce player passes and are available for Minecraft Bukkit/Forge/Vanilla and Minetest. Import/Bypass modes are available to help servers port their players to MinePass.

2. Players sign-up at minepass.net and Minecraft players verify their identity via our registration world

This allows MinePass to ensure a player is who they say they are. We operate on the player's UUID and are already prepared to handle future IGN changes. If a player changes their IGN with Mojang, they will be denied access to MinePass worlds until they repeat the verification process.

Minetest players need only a confirmed email address.

3. A player submits an application to a world, which may be manually or automatically reviewed

Worlds can customize the application process with custom fields, and at their option automatically issue a limited number of passes. Visitor's passes can even be time-limited.

4. World Moderators review and approve applications as needed

In addition to the information on the application, the world's moderators can review the player's profile which can contain commonly requested information.

5. If approved the player gains access to the world's portal and server details

Access to the world's servers and collaborative functions are made available once a player has a valid pass for the world.
